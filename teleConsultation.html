How should you book a video consultation through Cloudnine’s website?

Booking appointments through the online appointment booking widget on our website is easy, just follow these steps:-

  • You can go to  ‘Fix an appointment’ and Click on the option book online or use the widget present on this page
  • Select the City, Hospital, Department, Doctor, Procedure and Date 
  • Book a slot on the chosen date with your preferred doctor
  • Choose Video/Tele Consult
  • Fill in your mobile number and click on Get OTP
  • You’ll receive an OTP, fill that in the OTP bar
  • After which you’ll receive an option to ‘Book Now’
  • Following that, you can go ahead with finalising your booking
  • To start a video consultation,  It’s our baby app which you’ve already downloaded will be needed.

Your video consultation banner would be visible at the App home screen 30 min prior to the appointment.
